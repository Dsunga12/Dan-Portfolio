A school-level user management module for PRMS that allows principals to manage teacher accounts within their assigned school. Principals can view users, update account details, and edit or reset teacher passwords while keeping access limited to their own school.

Principal-level dashboard for school account monitoring View teacher users assigned to a specific school Update account details within the school scope Reset teacher passwords for account recovery Display teacher position, status, nature, and email Restrict access so principals only manage their own school
